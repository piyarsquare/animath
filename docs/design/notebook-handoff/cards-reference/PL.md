## note

Step to two coordinates. Addition rides along unchanged ([[AX]]). Multiplication,
under the same demands, collapses to a single number: what `j²` equals.

Call it `p`. One dial. Turn it and the whole arithmetic changes.

## full

A bilinear unital product on `ℝ²` is fixed by `j·j`, where `j = (0,1)`. Complete the
square and rescale `j` and it reads `j² = p` for a real `p`. The entire choice of
"how to multiply the plane" is that one real parameter. [[DV]]
