Seeing ez

A demo article hosting live animath applets in plain <iframe> tags

The exponential takes a point z = x + iy of the plane to e^z = u + iv. Below, the left pane is the input plane and the right pane is where every point lands — the same point keeps the same color, so you can follow it across.

Both planes are shadows of one four-dimensional object: the graph of every pair (z, e^z). The applet below shows that 4D graph. Drop Y projects out the input's imaginary axis, Drop X its real axis, and Rotate returns to the full 4D view, tumbling. Drag to orbit.
