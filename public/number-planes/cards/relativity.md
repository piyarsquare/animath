## note

The split plane's unit "rotation" slides along a hyperbola. That squeeze — fast
direction stretched, slow one shrunk, area kept — is exactly a Lorentz boost.
`x² − y²` is the spacetime interval.

## full

Split-complex numbers `x + jt` (`j² = 1`) carry the 1+1 Minkowski form `x² − t²`;
unit multiplication is a boost whose rapidity is the hyperbolic angle. Rapidities
add the way the split "angle" adds — the velocity-addition formula falls out. [[SP]]
