## note

Give each number a second coordinate — step into the plane. **Addition comes along
unchanged.** Multiplication does not: there's more than one way to multiply two
points.

Two questions run the rest:

- **Why are there choices — and what are they?**
- **What do the choices imply?**

One knob answers the first: what `j²` equals, a number `p`. Two ways to feel it — a
**3-way comparator** at `p = −1, 0, 1`, and a **single dial** that turns the plane
through every `p`.
