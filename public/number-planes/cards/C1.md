## note

On the line you do two things to a number: **add** it and **multiply** it. You can
*see* both — adding slides an arrow, multiplying stretches it, and flips it for a
negative.

Simple and familiar. But even here the rules aren't free: they're pinned by a few
quiet demands — which is where rings, fields, and the odd cousin *tropical* come in.
