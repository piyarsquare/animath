## note

Each choice of `p` is a whole world. Three of them:

- `p < 0` — **complex**: multiplying *turns* the plane (spin).
- `p = 0` — **dual**: it *shears*.
- `p > 0` — **split**: it *squeezes*.

The same count — how many real square roots `p` has — decides how many "rails" each
world has. And only one of the three lets you always divide: the complex plane.
