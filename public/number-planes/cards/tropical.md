## note

The line's rules were forced — *if you keep subtraction*. Drop it.

In tropical arithmetic "add" means **take the smaller** and "multiply" means
ordinary **add**: `3 ⊕ 5 = 3`, `3 ⊗ 5 = 8`. Consistent, useful — and there's no
subtracting your way back.

## full

`(ℝ ∪ {∞}, min, +)` is a semiring: `min` has no inverse, so it isn't a ring. It
still distributes, it linearizes hard optimization (shortest paths, scheduling), and
it underlies tropical geometry. The lesson: a system's rules are the price of the
guarantees you demand. [[AX]]
