## note

To deserve the name, a multiply should:

- **distribute** over addition, and
- have a **1** that changes nothing.

That's nearly all we ask. The surprise is how much it forces — and how little.

## full

Bilinearity (distributes on both sides) plus a unit; take commutative and
associative too. On the line these pin multiplication down completely. On the plane
they leave exactly one free number. The whole trail is the gap between "completely"
and "exactly one." [[L4]] [[PL]]
