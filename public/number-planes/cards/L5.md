## note

Call a number **stretchable** if something multiplies it back to `1` — if you can
divide by it. On the line the rule is dead simple: stretchable = not zero.

Hold the word. It decides more than it looks. [[higher]]

## full

Stretchable = invertible = a unit. Demanding *every* nonzero element be a unit is
demanding a field — what separates the complex plane from its siblings ([[FD]]) and,
raised one dimension at a time, what permits number systems only in dimensions
1, 2, 4, 8. [[higher]]
