## note

![[L5]]

Among the three, only the complex plane is stretchable through and through: every
nonzero number divides. The dual and split planes hold nonzero numbers you can't
undo (zero divisors). So only `p < 0` is a field.

## full

The norm `N_p = x² − p·y²` is multiplicative in all three. It's positive-definite
only for `p < 0` — there the sole non-unit is `0`, a field. For `p = 0` it's
degenerate (the `ε`-axis); for `p > 0` indefinite (a null cone): both carry zero
divisors. Demand a field in the plane and the dial is forced to `−1`. [[higher]]
