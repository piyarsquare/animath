## note

A complex number isn't a dot. It's a rule for combining points of a plane two ways:
how to **add** them, and how to **multiply** them.

Fix those two rules and you have the number system. Everything else is downstream.

## full

Formally: a set with `+` and `×` obeying the field axioms — an abelian group under
`+`, a group under `×` away from `0`, the two tied by distributivity. "What *is* a
complex number" is answered entirely by those two operations on `ℝ²`; the rest of
the subject is consequence. [[fields]] [[algebra]]
